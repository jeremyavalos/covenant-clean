What is Covenant?

Covenant is a habit and discipline app built around honest daily completion, 30-day progress, and reflective confrontation.

Is it free?

Yes. Free users can work with one habit. Covenant Pro unlocks multiple habits, deeper sessions, full history, and extended progress.

When will iOS/Android be available?

Covenant is preparing for launch on iOS and Android. Join the beta to be notified as access opens.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Account progress is saved securely so your habits, streaks, and completion history stay connected to your login.

Can I reset my password?

Yes. Covenant supports secure password reset by email for existing accounts.
